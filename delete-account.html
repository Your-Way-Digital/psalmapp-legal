Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your Psalm App account and associated data at any time by contacting us via email.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to contact@yourwaydigital.tech
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Account – Psalm App
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

What data is deleted: Your account, email address, profile information, favorites, notes, and purchase history linked to your account will be permanently deleted.

What is retained: Anonymized usage data may be retained for up to 90 days for legal and security compliance before being permanently erased.
